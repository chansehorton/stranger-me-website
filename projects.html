Capstone Project

The idea is a chrome extension that will provide a note-taking space on any given web page, and persist those notes, specific to that page, in localStorage. Stretch one is to add Google OAuth login, and to persist the notes to a db, allowing you to port notes to any computer. Stretch 2 is to enable voice notes.

Technologies: Chrome Extension framework, Postgres (or possibly a more google-native solution, voice-to-text translation library)
