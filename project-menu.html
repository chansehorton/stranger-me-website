Try Me!

A chrome extension that will provide a note-taking space on any given web page, and persist those notes, specific to that page, in localStorage. Stretch 1 is to add Google OAuth login, and to persist the notes to a db, allowing you to port notes to any computer. Stretch 2 is to enable voice notes.
